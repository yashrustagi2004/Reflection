About Reflection

Welcome to Reflection – your personal AI-powered companion for interview preparation. We understand that preparing for an interview can be stressful, time-consuming, and sometimes overwhelming. That's why we've created a platform that takes the guesswork out of preparation and helps you focus on what really matters: showcasing your skills and landing your dream job.

With Reflection, all you need to do is upload your resume and the job description for the role you’re targeting. Our intelligent system analyzes your profile and the requirements of the position to generate a set of customized interview questions just for you. No generic questions – only ones that truly help you grow.

Why You'll Love It

  • Smart & Personalized: Questions are tailored specifically to your resume and job description.
  • Analysis of Strengths & Weaknesses: Receive insightful feedback on your strengths and areas for improvement, helping you focus your preparation where it matters most.
  • Simple & Intuitive: A clean and engaging interface makes preparation stress-free and enjoyable.

Our Mission

At Reflection, we believe everyone deserves the chance to shine in their interviews. Our mission is to provide an intelligent, personalized, and user-friendly platform that empowers candidates to approach interviews with confidence and clarity. With Reflection, you’re not just practicing—you’re preparing strategically for success.

Whether you’re a fresh graduate stepping into the professional world, or an experienced professional aiming for your next big role, Reflection adapts to your needs and helps you perform at your best.
